How to use

  1. Click Upload Image and choose a photo, screenshot, or logo.
  2. Move the mouse over the image — a magnifier helps inspect small details.
  3. Click a point to read the pixel color and update HEX / RGB / HSL.
  4. Use Generate Palette to build a harmony from the active color.
  5. Adjust count, variation, and harmony mode.
  6. Click shades, gradients, or saved colors — each copies and activates the color.
  7. Use Copy CSS Variables to export the palette for your stylesheet.
